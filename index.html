Fluentear – English Listening Practice

Improve your listening skills from A1 to C1 with clear and structured content.

Level A1 (Beginner)

Focus: Survival English and very basic vocabulary.

  • Hello! My Basic World: greetings, farewells, introducing yourself, family, and household objects.
  • My Daily Life: simple routines, days of the week, time expressions, and basic leisure activities.
  • Where Is It?: places in the city, simple directions, food and drinks, and colors.

Level A2 (Elementary)

Focus: Talking about past and future experiences.

  • My Past and My Surroundings: simple biography, describing your city, weather and seasons.
  • Likes, Plans, and Experiences: hobbies, future plans, and telling simple past stories.
  • Daily Tasks and Health: shopping, ordering food, describing symptoms, parts of the body.

Level B1 (Intermediate)

Focus: Opening, maintaining, and closing conversations.

  • Stories and Personal Experiences: anecdotes, achievements, dreams, and life events.
  • Opinions and Advice: expressing opinions on everyday topics and giving advice politely.
  • Culture and Lifestyle: comparing cultures, habits, and discussing future goals.

Level B2 (Upper-Intermediate)

Focus: Fluent expression of complex and abstract ideas.

  • Debates and Arguments: advantages, disadvantages, defending a point of view.
  • Relationships and Communication: non-verbal language, idioms, social interaction.
  • Culture and Society: trends, media analysis, ethics, and values.

Level C1 (Advanced)

Focus: Precise, fluent, and nuanced use of the language.

  • Abstract and Specialized Topics: philosophy, politics, science, globalisation.
  • Figurative Language and Humor: idioms, sarcasm, wordplay, cultural humor.
  • Critical Thinking and Persuasion: persuasive presentations, rhetorical analysis.
